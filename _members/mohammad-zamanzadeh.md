My research interests lie at the intersection of biosignal processing and machine learning, with a particular focus on Bayesian modeling. My work aims to develop normative models of electrophysiological signals as a pathway toward precision psychiatry. As part of the ML4PNP lab, I am involved in developing MEGaNorm, an end-to-end pipeline for deriving lifespan normative ranges and performing individual-level analyses of M/EEG data.
