We are pleased to welcome [Melissa Zandstra](https://ml4pnp.github.io/members/melissa-zandstra.html) to the ML4PNP lab as a **postdoctoral researcher**.

Melissa will lead the project **“Normative Range of P600 Morphology as a Diagnostic and Prognostic Tool in Neuropsychiatric Disorders.”** This project is funded within the **NWO NWA – Research along Routes** programme. The project aims to establish normative ranges for the **P600**, an event-related potential associated with language processing, using large-scale EEG data and advanced computational methods.More information about the project can be found on the [NWO project page](https://www.nwo.nl/projecten/vqlab92202).

We are excited to have Melissa join the lab and look forward to the contributions her work will bring to the ML4PNP research program.
